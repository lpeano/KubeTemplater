# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us as soon as possible. We take all security vulnerabilities seriously and will work with you to understand and fix the issue.

To report a security vulnerability, please send an email to [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS] with a detailed description of the vulnerability and steps to reproduce it.

We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours and will work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible. We will keep you informed of our progress and will notify you when the vulnerability has been fixed.

We appreciate your help in keeping our project secure.
